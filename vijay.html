Vijay

Actor Vijay, whose full name is Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, is a popular Indian actor, film producer, and playback singer primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. He was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Vijay is often referred to as "Thalapathy" (which means "commander" or "leader" in Tamil) by his fans due to his immense popularity. He made his acting debut in 1992 with the film *Naalaiya Theerpu* and gained widespread recognition with hits like *Ghilli* (2004), *Kavalan* (2011), *Thuppakki* (2012), *Mersal* (2017), and *Master* (2021). Known for his charismatic screen presence, dancing skills, and versatility in various roles, Vijay has become one of the biggest stars in Tamil cinema. In addition to his acting career, Vijay is involved in philanthropy and is known for his charitable work, particularly in supporting education and healthcare for underprivileged people. His fan base is enormous, and he enjoys a massive following not only in Tamil Nadu but also among Tamil-speaking communities worldwide.
